SSAC. Allura Sigrath

The woman who would become known as The Shadow Master

Here's a timeline of Allura Sigrath life:

  • 1920born in the small town of Tarx, located in the Maravene province of the country of Ocs Ano.
  • 1932the year she was accepted into the military academy of Gahl'vuhr Sehn for her exceptional talent in shadow magic.
  • 1936graduated top of her class and was immediately deployed to the Arat province as part of the Hiqin province pacification force.
  • 1941transferred to the Delerg province and made a captain at age 21.
  • 1947recruited by the Eternal Dominion and transferred to the Gahl'vuhr Sehn intelligence office.
  • 1949in Ragoran operation, managed to thwart a rebellion attempt lead by the notorious criminal Rambton.
  • 1951single-handedly uncovered an anti-government conspiracy by the infamous Trelik the Modist.
  • 1954infiltrated the city of Rask and was responsible for the arrest of several high-ranking corrupt government officials, including the governor Fel.
  • 1955was promoted to royal shadow agent of the first rank.
  • 1959led a raid on the so-called 'neutral' city of Zal, during the course of which she destroyed several anti-edge war machines.
  • 1964Trelik the Modist's organization, the Nocturnal League, was completely eradicated.
  • 1965promoted to shadow master of the first rank.
  • 1971assumed leadership of the Royal Shadows following the disappearance of their leader, Ereds Haran.
  • 1973successfully repelled a major Felian counter-attack.
  • 1976eliminated the last remnants of the Conglomerate, the anti-constitutional organization, with the death of Zamed II.
  • 1977was declared a hero of the Empire for her works in the 'Secret War'.

"From shadow to shadow, from life to death, I am the agent of the shadow. Embrace the darkness within. Embrace the power within. For the darkness is our light. For it is a gift I was bestowed upon. It is a duty I must fulfill."

-- Shadow agents oath of initiation
